##

The lunge is a unilateral lower body exercise that develops quadriceps, gluteal, and hamstring strength while challenging balance and coordination. Starting from a standing position with dumbbells at the sides, the lifter takes a step forward and lowers the body until both knees are bent at approximately 90 degrees, with the back knee hovering just above the floor. Key technique: maintain an upright torso, knee aligned with the toes (no valgus collapse), front foot flat throughout, and drive through the front heel to return to standing.
