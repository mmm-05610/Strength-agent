##

The Romanian Deadlift (RDL) emphasizes the eccentric phase of the hip hinge pattern, primarily targeting the hamstrings and glutes. Unlike the conventional deadlift (which starts from the floor), the RDL starts from the standing position. Key technique: with a slight knee bend (~20 degrees), hinge at the hips and push the buttocks backward while maintaining a neutral spine, lower the bar along the front of the thighs until a stretch is felt in the hamstrings (typically mid-shin level for most individuals), then return to standing by driving the hips forward.
