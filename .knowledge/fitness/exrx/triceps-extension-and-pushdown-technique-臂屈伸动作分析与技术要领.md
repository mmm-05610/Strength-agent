##

Triceps isolation exercises target the triceps brachii (lateral, medial, and long heads), which constitutes approximately two-thirds of upper arm mass. Key variations: cable pushdown (constant tension, most accessible), lying triceps extension/skull crusher (overhead position stretches the long head, maximizing its recruitment), overhead cable extension (deep stretch on long head), close-grip bench press (compound-emphasis variant), and dips (bodyweight compound variant, high triceps activation when performed upright). Key technique: maintain stable shoulder position, control the full range of motion, avoid elbow flare, and use a controlled eccentric (2 seconds).
