   **Hand Spacing**

- A grip width of approximately 1.5-2 times biacromial width (shoulder width) is recommended
- Wider grip emphasizes pectoralis activation

- Press the bar back toward the face/upper chest area
- This curved bar path reduces shoulder impingement risk

## Execution Phases

## Execution Phases

**Eccentric Phase (Lowering)**

- Duration: 2-3 seconds
- Elbows at approximately 45-75 degrees from the torso
- Wrists stacked directly over elbows
- Inhale during the descent
- Bar touches the chest lightly (no bouncing)
  **Transition (Amortization Phase)**
- Brief pause at the chest may enhance power output
- Maintain full body tension
- Do not relax at the bottom position
  **Concentric Phase (Pressing)**
- Explosive press from the chest
- Maintain the five-point contact
- Exhale through the sticking point
- Full elbow extension at lockout without hyperextension
