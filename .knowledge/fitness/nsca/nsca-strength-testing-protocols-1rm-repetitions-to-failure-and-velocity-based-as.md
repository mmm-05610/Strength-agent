## Estimating 1RM from Repetitions-to-Failure

## Estimating 1RM from Repetitions-to-Failure

For novice or deconditioned clients, estimate 1RM from submaximal performance to avoid the risks of maximal testing.
**Common formulas**:

- Brzycki: 1RM = weight × (36 / (37 - reps))
- Epley: 1RM = weight × (1 + reps/30)
- Used for reps in the 2-10 range (accuracy decreases with >10 reps)
  **Protocol**: After warm-up, select a weight the client can lift for 5-10 repetitions to failure with good technique. Apply formula. Test 2-3 core exercises (not every exercise in a session).
