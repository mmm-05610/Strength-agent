## The RAMP Warm-Up Framework

The NSCA recommends the RAMP framework (Raise, Activate, Mobilize, Potentiate) for structuring effective warm-ups.

## R - Raise

**Goal**: Elevate body temperature, heart rate, respiration rate, and blood flow to working muscles.
**Duration**: 5-8 minutes
**Activities**: Light jogging, stationary cycling, rowing, skipping rope
**Target**: Light sweat onset, HR elevated to ~60-70% HRmax
**Physiological effect**: Muscle temperature increase of 1-2°C improves power output by 2-5% and reduces injury risk.

## A - Activate

## M - Mobilize

**Goal**: Move joints through the full range of motion required in training. Dynamic (not static) movements.
**Duration**: 3-5 minutes
**Key mobility drills**:

- World's greatest stretch (hip flexor + thoracic rotation)
- Leg swings (forward/back and side-to-side)
- Arm circles and shoulder dislocates with band
- Hip circles and cat-cow for spine
