# Resistance Training Frequency: Effects on Strength and Hypertrophy in Novices

权威等级: 高
内容类型: 运动科学文献
发表年份: 2024

## Abstract

## Abstract

This 12-week RCT randomized 96 novice participants (45 male, 51 female, age 22.3±3.1 years) into three training frequency groups: 1x/week (total body, high volume per session), 2x/week (upper/lower split), and 3x/week (push/pull/legs split). Total weekly volume was equated across groups (15 sets/muscle group/week). Results showed: all groups significantly increased strength (p<0.001), with 2x/week producing the largest effect (ES=0.89 vs 0.71 for 1x/week and 0.85 for 3x/week, no significant between-group differences for the primary outcome). Hypertrophy measured via ultrasound (vastus lateralis thickness) showed similar gains across groups (ES=0.42-0.51, no significant differences).
